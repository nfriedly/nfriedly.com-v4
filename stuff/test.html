Flowers

Celebrate any occasion with fresh flowers from FTD. Here, you will find only the highest quality flower arrangements and floral bouquets - perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, or any holiday throughout the year. Better yet, FTD is always fast, easy, and convenient, ready to serve you 24 hours a day and 7 days a week. Your gift will be hand-delivered by one of our professional FTD florists, to ensure a timely arrival and an impeccable experience for your recipient.

Nothing could be simpler than ordering our flowers online - and whether it's Valentine’s Day, Mother's Day, Christmas, a birthday, or just because, FTD offers the perfect gift for all occasions. Featuring gourmet foods, plants, jewelry, and much more, FTD has a wide variety of products for all the special people in your life. Explore flowers and gift options in a variety of price ranges, and discover many offerings that are within your budget.

Send flowers from FTD, and feel confident that you’re making your recipient happy. You can trust that the quality will be without peer, whether you’ve ordered a bouquet of beautiful roses, vibrant tulips, impressive lilies, or lovely Gerbera daisies.

Valentine's Day Flowers

Valentine's Day is approaching, and FTD has everything you need to help you celebrate with someone you love. Valentine's Day flowers and bouquets always send a perfect message, and you're sure to find just the right thing in our collection of Valentine's Day bestselling gifts.

You can never go wrong with Valentine roses from FTD. Celebrate the most romantic day of the year with the nation's most trusted florist, and we'll show you why so many people rely on FTD for special occasions year-round.

Same Day Flower Delivery

When waiting is not an option, you can get flowers delivered same day from an artisan FTD florist. Order by 2 P.M. in your recipient’s time zone for same day delivery, Monday through Friday.

We guarantee that our beautiful floral arrangements and plants will last at least seven days. In addition, FTD.com can deliver flowers around the world. International deliveries can be made the next business day when you place your order by 2 P.M. Eastern Time.

Payment is a breeze as well - we accept Paypal, Bill Me Later, and all major credit cards, so you can get what you need however you like.

Perfect Gifts and Flower Arrangements

Whatever the reason, you can rely on FTD for all your gift needs and as always, beautiful flowers that are fresh and affordable.
